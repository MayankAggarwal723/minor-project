Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How quickly can I get an ambulance in an emergency?

Our average ambulance response time is under 10 minutes in urban areas. We use GPS technology to dispatch the nearest available ambulance to your location.

Are all doctors on FastMedico verified?

Yes, every doctor on our platform undergoes a rigorous verification process including license validation, qualification checks, and background verification.

Can I use FastMedico without creating an account?

While you can browse our services without an account, you'll need to create a free account to book appointments, order medicines, or use our emergency services.

How accurate is the bed availability information?

Our bed availability data is updated in real-time directly from our partner hospitals, ensuring you get the most current information available.

Is my medical information secure with FastMedico?

Absolutely. We use bank-level encryption and comply with all healthcare data protection regulations to ensure your medical information remains private and secure.
